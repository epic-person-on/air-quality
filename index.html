The air we breathe

Did you know that air pollution is one of the leading causes of respiratory diseases and premature death? We're committed to raising awareness and promoting change to improve air quality in our communities.

What you can do

  • Reduce car use and switch to public transport or cycling
  • Avoid burning wood or rubbish
  • Choose environmentally-friendly products
  • Support clean energy initiatives
